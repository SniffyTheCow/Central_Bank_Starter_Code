4.      Trade bonus decision.

If you are selected to be the decision-maker, you will be asked: “Do you want to assign the trade bonus to the red player?” You can choose YES or NO. 

- If you choose “YES,” then the red player earns the entire trade bonus.

- Otherwise, half of the trade bonus is destroyed, and you keep the other half for yourself.
