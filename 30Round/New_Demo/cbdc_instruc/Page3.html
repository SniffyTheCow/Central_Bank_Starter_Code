2.      Trade choice. 

You choose to either trade or not. To make a trade choice you must click on the desired button and then click “submit.” See the figure below.

 

                                                  TRADE       DO NOT TRADE

SUBMIT

 

- By choosing TRADE you will complete 1 trade with each group member who also chooses to trade.

- By choosing DO NOT TRADE you will forgo 1 trade with each group member.


You will earn 30 points for each trade you forego. Every other participant will also receive 30 points each, as they forego one trade with you.


Instead, you will earn 29 points from each trade you complete. If you are red, each trade you complete also creates a 50-point trade bonus. You may or may not end up keeping those bonus points, as we explain below.
