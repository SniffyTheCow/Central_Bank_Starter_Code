In this part we scale up the group to 6 and we play a few more rounds; there will be a predetermined number of periods (at least 18) that is not disclosed at this point. 

At the end of the session the computer will randomly select one of the periods that you will play. The points that you have earned in that selected period will be your payment for part 3. Each point is worth 2.5 cents.
